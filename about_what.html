BOB-FM is the "play anything" format.

BOB-FM breaks with the traditions of existing radio programming and has a play list 3 or 4 times larger than those formats. Listeners have long said they wanted to hear more of variety in the mix of radio stations. They have several and varying tastes in music and they keep punching the dial whenever their mood changes. With BOB-FM songs rotate much more slowly, eliminating the repetition of other stations. BOB-FM is a format both men and women will listen to, together. It targets the highly sought after 25-54 age group and (does but) doesn't play favorites either (pun intended) attracting both men and women in like numbers.

BOB-FM plays the pop and rock based music you heard in the 70's, 80's and 90's. If it was ever a Top 40 Hit you will probably find it on BOB-FM.

This unconventional, unpredictable format is cutting edge creating the latest genre of music presentation. The listeners are excited, and we'll bet if they're excited you will be too.
